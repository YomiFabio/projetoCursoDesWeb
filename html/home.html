OUR RESTAURANT

10 years ago Cafe Fontenebleau was born! Since we started our activities, we aim to provide a unique experience to our guests.

Our entire menu consists of traditional Mexican dishes, prepared by native chefs.

We work with the rotation system or a la carte, the first option being the great success of the house.

Take a Closer Look

Our private dining room provides a warm, intimate setting for your event. We can accommodate 60 seated guests for dinner or up to 100 guests for a standing reception.
